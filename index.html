Engine Options

Three personalities. One legend

M3 (Base)

  • Power: 473 hp (353 kW)
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Manual
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive

The standard M3 offers an engaging manual gearbox and classic rear-wheel drive dynamics. It's the purist's choice — raw, balanced, and incredibly rewarding to drive.

Learn More

M3 Competition

  • Power: 503 hp (375 kW)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed M Steptronic
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel or xDrive AWD

The M3 Competition raises the bar with more power, faster shifts, and the option for BMW's advanced xDrive all-wheel system. It's built for both precision and performance.

Learn More

M3 CS

  • Power: 543 hp (405 kW)
  • Transmission: 8-Speed M Steptronic
  • Drivetrain: M xDrive AWD

The M3 CS is the ultimate evolution — lighter, sharper, and even more powerful. Carbon fiber components and exclusive tuning make it a track-ready monster for the streets.

Design & Aerodynamics

Every line of the M3 G80 serves a purpose — balancing aesthetics and downforce.

Form Meets Excellence

The M3 G80's design is not just for show; it's engineered for performance. Every curve and vent is optimized for airflow, reducing drag and enhancing stability at high speeds.

Crafted with precision, the M3 G80 blends advanced materials and bold styling to achieve perfect aerodynamic harmony. From its sculpted hood to the rear diffuser, every detail enhances handling, cooling, and road presence — a true fusion of form and function.
